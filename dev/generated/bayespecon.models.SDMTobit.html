    bayespecon.models.SDMTobit¶

    class bayespecon.models.SDMTobit(*args, censoring=0.0, **kwargs)[source]¶

    Bayesian spatial Durbin Tobit model.

    \[y^* = \rho Wy^* + X\beta_1 + WX\beta_2 + \varepsilon, \quad \varepsilon \sim N(0,\sigma^2 I)\]

    with observed outcome y = max(c, y*).

    spatial_effects()[source]¶

    Compute posterior direct, indirect, and total impacts.

    Models without a spatial lag on y do not exhibit global feedback propagation through \((I- ho W)^{-1}\). However, models with spatially lagged covariates (SLX, SDEM) can still have non-zero neighbour spillovers captured in the indirect term.

    Returns:¶

    • dict with keys “direct”, “indirect”, “total”, each shaped

    • (n_features,) containing posterior mean impacts.
