    "# Nonlinear Spatial Models\n",
    "\n",
    "This guide demonstrates three nonlinear spatial models in `bayespecon` on synthetic data.\n",
    "\n",
    "## Spatial Probit\n",
    "\n",
    "For binary outcomes $y_i \\in \\{0,1\\}$, latent utilities $y^*$ follow a spatial lag:\n",
    "\n",
    "$$y^* = \\rho W y^* + X\\beta + a + \\varepsilon, \\quad \\varepsilon \\sim \\mathcal{N}(0, I)$$\n",
    "\n",
    "where $a$ are region-level random effects and $y_i = \\mathbf{1}[y^*_i > 0]$.\n",
    "\n",
    "## SAR Tobit\n",
    "\n",
    "For censored outcomes, the spatial autoregressive Tobit specifies:\n",
    "\n",
    "$$y^* = \\rho W y^* + X\\beta + \\varepsilon, \\quad \\varepsilon \\sim \\mathcal{N}(0, \\sigma^2 I)$$\n",
    "$$y_i = \\max(c,\\, y^*_i)$$\n",
    "\n",
    "where $c$ is the censoring threshold (default 0).\n",
    "\n",
    "## SEM Tobit\n",
    "\n",
    "The spatial error Tobit places spatial dependence in the disturbance:\n",
    "\n",
    "$$y^* = X\\beta + u, \\quad u = \\lambda W u + \\varepsilon, \\quad \\varepsilon \\sim \\mathcal{N}(0, \\sigma^2 I)$$\n",
    "$$y_i = \\max(c,\\, y^*_i)$$"
   ]

    "\n",
    "rng = np.random.default_rng(1234)"

    "# SpatialProbit\n",
    "m, n_per_region = 8, 20\n",
    "\n",
    "# Create line-graph weights: each node connected to neighbors\n",
    "focal, neighbor = [], []\n",
    "for i in range(m):\n",
    "    if i > 0:\n",
    "        focal.append(i)\n",
    "        neighbor.append(i - 1)\n",
    "    if i < m - 1:\n",
    "        focal.append(i)\n",
    "        neighbor.append(i + 1)\n",
    "Wm = Graph.from_arrays(\n",
    "    np.array(focal), np.array(neighbor), weight=np.ones(len(focal))\n",
    ").transform(\"r\")\n",
    "\n",
    "rho, beta, sigma_a = 0.35, np.array([0.3, 1.0]), 0.8\n",
    "\n",
    "sp_data = dgp.simulate_spatial_probit(\n",
    "    W=Wm,\n",
    "    rho=rho,\n",
    "    beta=beta,\n",
    "    sigma_a=sigma_a,\n",
    "    n_per_region=n_per_region,\n",
    "    rng=rng,\n",
    ")\n",
    "\n",
    "sp = SpatialProbit(\n",
    "    y=sp_data[\"y\"],\n",
    "    X=sp_data[\"X\"],\n",
    "    W=sp_data[\"W_graph\"],\n",
    "    region_ids=sp_data[\"region_ids\"],\n",
    ")\n",
    "sp.fit(draws=300, tune=300, chains=2, random_seed=42, progressbar=False)\n",
    "sp.summary(var_names=[\"rho\", \"beta\", \"sigma_a\"])"
   ]
